About Wearthy

Wearthy is a platform for showcasing verified sustainable fashion brands, with a focus on start-ups and small businesses in Central and Eastern Europe (CEE) and the DACH region.

Our Mission

To accelerate the ethical transformation of the fashion industry by giving visibility to emerging sustainable brands and connecting them with conscious consumers.
